The Power of Flying

You step onto the roof of a tall building, take a nervous breath, and jump. For a second you are terrified… but then you feel the air holding you up. You are really flying.

You glide above the city, watching tiny cars and tiny people far below. You can visit your friends in seconds, skip every traffic jam, and see every sunset from the best possible angle in the sky.

But there is a small problem: you can never hide anymore. Everyone points their phones at you, recording videos of the “flying human.” News channels want interviews, governments want answers, and you cannot go anywhere without being recognized.

You have an amazing view of the world, but almost no privacy left.

Start over and choose again.
